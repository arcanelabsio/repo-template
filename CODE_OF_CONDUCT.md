# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Arcane Labs is a place where contributors — maintainers, users, first-time PR-authors — collaborate on code that other people depend on. We pledge to make participation in our projects a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or opinion about tabs vs. spaces.

## Standards

Behaviors that contribute to a positive environment:

- Focus on what's best for the project and its users
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Show empathy and patience toward other contributors
- Credit contributors publicly; criticize ideas privately first when possible

Behaviors that are unacceptable:

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind — public or private
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would be reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **apps@arcanelabs.info**. Reports are handled confidentially.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate action in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful. This may include warning the offender, temporary or permanent bans, or public statements when the situation warrants transparency.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, commit messages) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
